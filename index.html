    The Arc

    The Climb

    Four nights or seven — the arc is the same.

    1. Arrival · The trailhead

      Exhale. You're here.

      The jungle slows you down before you even unpack. A welcome dinner at sunset. The first breath of real air.

    2. Opening · First switchbacks

      The day is yours.

      Morning movement on the open shala, ocean on the horizon. Something in you starts to unclench.

    3. The Edge · The steep pitch

      This is the day.

      The moment you realize this isn't comfortable. It's transformative. Breathwork. Cold water. A version of you that's been waiting.

    4. Connection · The ridge line

      The conversations you'll replay for years.

      Long tables. Real questions. The kind of laughter that resets something.

    5. Integration · The high camp

      Something has changed.

      You can feel it but not yet name it. That's exactly right.

    6. Return · The summit at dawn

      You leave with allies.

      And a version of yourself you're excited to meet.

    Questions, answered

    Who is this retreat for?

    People at a turning point — a career, a relationship, a decade. High performers who are tired of performing. If you read this far, probably you.

    Do I need experience with yoga or breathwork?

    No. Every practice is guided and every level is welcome. Willingness matters more than flexibility.

    Can I come alone?

    Yes. Most people do.

    How big is the group?

    Every group is curated by application. Who's in the room matters more to us than how many.

    Is this religious or spiritual?

    No religion, no dogma. Breathwork, stillness, movement, and cold water — explained in plain language, grounded in practice.

    What happens after I apply?

    A real person reads your application. We respond within 48 hours. Applying commits you to nothing — it starts a conversation.

    What if I can't make these dates?

    Join the list at the bottom of this page and you'll hear when the next retreat opens.

    What's the refund policy?

    Full transparency before you pay anything: the policy is shared in writing during your acceptance call, before any payment is made.
